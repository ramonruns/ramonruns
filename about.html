About Me

Ramon

Runner · Zug, Switzerland

Hi, I'm Ramon — a passionate runner based in Zug. I started running already in my early days. Born in 1997 and probably running by 1999 already. In my childhood years I was part of LK Zug and the middle distance events such as 800m / 1000m / 1500m have always been my favourite disciplines.

After retiring from the track I started participating at road races — 10k races up to Half Marathons. My first Half Marathon was in 2014 at 17. As part of a school project in my Masters Studies at PH Luzern I planned and completed my first marathon in 2021. Four years later I ran my first official Marathon in a race in sub 3:30. Hungry for more!

My main goal for 2026 is to break 3:15 at SwissCity Marathon Lucerne in October. Every run and every race is a step in that direction.
